Privacy Policy for DailyCart - Shop & Note Last updated: May 2026 Welcome to DailyCart - Shop & Note, developed by Vicky Technologies. DailyCart is a shopping list and notes application that helps users manage daily tasks, create notes, organize shopping lists, and securely store personal information. The app can be used both online and offline. Information We Collect DailyCart does not require account registration or login. We do not collect, store, or sell personal information such as: Full name Phone number Email address Payment information Personal notes or shopping lists All user-created content, including notes, locked notes, hidden notes, and shopping lists, is stored locally on the user’s device. Even when the app is connected to the internet, user data remains stored on the local device and is not uploaded to our servers. App Usage DailyCart can be used both online and offline. Internet access may be used for: Displaying advertisements Loading optional online content or services Improving app functionality However, personal notes and shopping data remain stored only on the user’s device. Locked & Hidden Notes DailyCart may offer privacy features such as: Locked Notes Hidden Notes Local Privacy Protection These features are designed to help users keep personal content private on their own device. DailyCart does not access, read, or upload locked or hidden notes to external servers. Users are responsible for remembering passwords, PINs, or lock methods used within the app. Advertisements DailyCart may display third-party advertisements while connected to the internet. The app may use advertising services provided by Google AdMob. These services may collect limited non-personal information such as: Device information Advertising ID App interaction data This information is used for ad delivery and performance measurement. Vicky Technologies does not store user notes, shopping lists, or personal content on external servers. Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their devices.
